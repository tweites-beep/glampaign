How to use Glampaign

Add shots: Drag any node from the left sidebar onto the canvas.

Upload images: Click or drop onto the image area of any Shot node.

Connect nodes: Click the ● dot on the right of one node, drag to the ● dot on the left of another.

Generate: Hit Run on any AI node — it reads everything connected to it. Connect your shots into a Sizzle Reel node for the final sequence.

Shortcuts: V select · H pan · Delete remove · +− zoom · F fit view
